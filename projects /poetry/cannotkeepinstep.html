Cannot keep in step: July 19, 2009



let me give you this one new page

where old pages end
where know what lay forgotten was so not
forgotten not and could be
when oceans shine sunrise
take the gold lift from the waters
rise and let be that pass you over us
back past back left what had fair
had left behind

Where will our haunted sorrow go?
I dare not blame you
so come of banished sorrow not less I blame you
come from altered and forgotten mountains

For only did this moment I realize
for you to be with like dream to my life
less no life you bring to another
less not no infant life and a life of another kind

Fair be not that the sun did rise today
forever it will rise and over the ocean

Fair that love did banish me
for all that I am it did banished
the revolution of the earth and season meant nothing to be

So, for when others can lavish in their desires I cannot
they lavish because they can and it feels good to their emotion
so, but they have a lifetime of it
taken from me -so now that I command the texture of it-

Where some lives can lavish in the experience of emotion
I create it and so to for will I always make that choice to

So, but when and where others are rewarded for their love
with bonds, and so are rewarded with life and infants, children to love
They grow old having filled themselves
I cannot have hate for them
for then to would my forgiveness of my own love be a contradiction

Space and time cross out and reach by day or moon light,
and only fairly speak I can for I cannot see and if I can it fails to make sense

For all that I am cannot neither be not not be made sense of
how can that be
how could we let go
let go
throw it to the floor
and let be

someone who cannot be
for that cross the lake fresh waters there is some like place and life I cannot compete with
I can not compete with cast mirages and empathy and empty theories.

who would choose metaphysics over a family
because having that kind of love
nothing can be like it
not good love can my love ever be

my dreams have such movements in moments but my art in step cannot keep
