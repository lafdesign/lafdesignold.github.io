Extinguish: May 17th 2009




Forever realized by nothing but ash and electricity,
water and sunlight and my paper
ground of being
rest in certainty

Dead and be buried,
or grown into something different
Evermore bloodless residue
colorless and non-human
odorless, spiritless and blanched
baked bleach and snow-blindness bright
broken down crushed into vapor

Respire something, take a breath
suddenly and on one's own accord
tend to the fire that puts forth the light
so we can see and be warm

Did the wind you meet?
Did you catch a storm on a ship at sea?
Lean into my ear, you can speak to me
If you need, I will orate for you;
lay shadows behind me if you are the light I see-

Would be lost but I find you
would be but are saved.
would have been willed to be destroyed
But god did not smile, you did

I betray only my self now
the birds do not move any more
they flap their wings,
caught immobile in a breeze

Time and space be and so be caught up
twisted and bent out of line
alone by the sun, by the water the moon
let alone by you and by me.
did our frames not cascade?
did our metaphors coincide?

I am here to see the sun with you
the moon can be our child
the stars can be
our eyes
other matters far out of reach
yet they reach to me
and they signify you
here on this planet
I will find you
when the sun rises and the moon sets
when the stars are right
aligned to the ash in me

the ground at my feet was spinning before it even coalesced
from whence it was dust
there we were, there we met
Here we have always been

How you left me soft spoken
In a valley with a gossamer mist
that was pain to me
my deepest fear is that it will pain forever you too

the rain began falling to bring in the night
extinguish the fire the warmth and the light
