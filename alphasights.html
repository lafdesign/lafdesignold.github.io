Whatever the role, whether a graduate Analyst in Hong Kong, a Senior Associate in New York or a Software Developer in London, you’ll find our people all have the following attributes in common:

Initiative. Success doesn’t just happen. It is not earned through entitlement or previous experiences, but hard work and persistence. Successful people spot opportunities and have the creativity and resourcefulness to make it happen.

Drive. Successful individuals possess infectious energy and optimism. Our people are always looking to raise the bar and challenge the status quo.

Teamwork. We understand that we are stronger together. Which is why we look for people who love working in teams and will always put camaraderie before personal gain. We help where we can and trust each other to get things done.

Curiosity. We look for people who are passionate about understanding the world and the people around them. Individuals who are never afraid to fail and are always looking to learn.

Confidence. We look for individuals with the ability to build instant rapport and long-lasting relationships. At AlphaSights, we believe in ourselves and never give up, whatever the challenge.
