Intention driven ropes today: December 7th, 2009



-Reject or be forced to contradict oneself-
The pattern can be deduced, reduced and distinguished
-but to see,
-is a matter of happening
co-fated with the decision you make
-(the un-coiling of the rope)
-the unwinding of a chain from a wench
links in place, knots
ready to take place as time marches on
not going to give you an answer
Did that train derail for no reason what so ever?
maybe you could not have done of yourself that you did

What you favor you watch out for;
as I watch you
predict your motions
I imagine fated for a better place
Not one that their words make out
there was no train to take you,
walk the hill sides, climb up, descend, suspend in time if you are late
become enlightened -by them-
