The Muse iOS App

The Muse iOS App is a new way to enjoy art on your phone.

Browse beautiful paintings in a uniquely visual way. Save your favorite paintings, learn and discover new artists.

Access a growing database of over 100,000 paintings by the best contemporary artists and the old master, updated daily. Join a uniquely visual community of art and art lovers.

2,000 friends asked Muse to access our content on iOS.

We built a very visual app, while enabling the user to dig into our database of contemporary, modern and classic art.
