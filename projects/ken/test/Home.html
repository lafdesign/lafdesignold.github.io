Welcome!

 

SketchUp is an amazing application!

It is a most dynamic way to bring your ideas to life in the shortest amount of time.

You can create freely on the fly!

It is intuitive, straightforward and fun to use!

 

As a production designer in the film industry I have been using SketchUp for well over 8 years.

All the imagery on this site I created using SketchUp.

I have used SketchUp on television shows such as Cashmere Mafia, Believe, Forever, and now Saturday Night Live.

Having designed hundreds of commercials including Google, Capital One and Garnier,

I have also used SketchUp for architects, interior designers, decorators and home builders.

 

Presentation is everything! Everything!

It's the difference between landing or NOT landing a client.

If you're an interior designer, architect, builder, set designer, designer or a decorator,

I will teach you all the tips and tricks I’ve learned that will make a tremendous difference in your learning curve

and the way you approach and think about SketchUp.

Design is about creating, not struggling.

 

My class is about creating the confidence that will enable you to convincingly express your ideas

and present them in your most creative and professional way.
