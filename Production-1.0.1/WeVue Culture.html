THE CULTURE APP : CAPTURE, ENHANCE, AND SHOWCASE YOUR COMPANY CULTURE WITH THE HELP OF YOUR EMPLOYEES

A PRIVATE CULTURE NETWORK FOR YOUR COMPANY

Communicating and collaborating with photos, videos, and text will change the way you:



  •   Introduce new hires


  •   Announce sales wins


  •   Showcase your employer brand to new recruit


  •   Share company events


  •     Broadcast what’s happening with video and push notifications

A BRANDED APP FOR YOUR PEOPLE

Once your employees log in with their company email, the app is yours



  •   Custom theme with a color scheme to match your branding


  •   Secure and only accessible by your employees


  •   Flexible privacy settings to fit your social media policies


  •   Connects disparate workforces visually


  •   Hold contests to engage employees anywhere, anytime

A CONTENT MARKETING TOOL TO TELL YOUR STORIES

The people that work with you have tales to tell - leverage them to gather:



  •   Customer testimonials


  •   Product demos


  •   Cultural content for recruiting purposes


  •   Highlights from trade shows and expos


  •   Visuals that show what your company is all about

SHOWCASE YOUR COMPANY CULTURE WITH THE PEOPLE WHO MAKE YOUR STORIES

all of the content added to the app can be edited and selectively shared to the public


  •    On your website: drop a few lines of code into any site, and easily moderate the content that goes from the app to your company culture page, or anywhere else on your website.

  •    On social media: photos and videos shared from the app on social media drive traffic to a branded page that links to any relevant site about you company.
