so, act now: December 4th, 2009



To realize means to create:
So I was dreaming
I was trying to save you from a bad-life
the kind that haunts my sleep at night
but no good-life I can provide you

-but you're not the one who waits awake,
you try
and you succeeded and I am proud of you
but what you have is not always the one you want
so at the end of the day
when you go to fall asleep
when it comes down to something you can count on
I don't think there's any thing at all
(I am the dark)

I tried the best that I had to try for
nothing can give back to me
the million tons of chemicals that my brain had to make
those pathways I wired
but if they change and I forget
I am the only one who knows how hard it its to be
don't hesitate to stop and change your course
the world will not wait
your time, made with the most, of what you got
it could be great
a little bit of this and that
and spark it up
roll it out parade
again and over and
you'll be there
and you'll be afraid
and we will always be there
and the feeling will subdue you
from the ashes we will rise up and take our place
for the rest of time
usher in the new age paradigm
it won't be there if we wait,
so, act now!
