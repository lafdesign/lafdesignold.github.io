Phi-losophy: May 15th 2009



If I am not frightened
If I am not sad
If nor am I mad
Then what is fear?
What is sadder or madder?
but a brush of electricity
the electromagnetic storm of a dream
the corporal grip of a nightmare
Happy birthday my sorrow
one year older my fear
how my sadness went quickly

When it comes to perception its hard to say there is any sense about it.
It breaks my heart it rips at me, its got me in its hold
I am cold
Iâ€™m cold blooded
cold hearted
selfish and old
my mind becomes weary

Absolute certainty that never is going anywhere
who are you ?
I am but a participle for a greater action
So, but the part of me that wills, to be
is frightened
frightened solely from what can be imagined
nothing cant be
everything is continued upon my being
when the justifier is external
I am not mine
and, so to be predicated by another
forever realized by nothing but ash and electricity,
water and sunlight and my paper
ground of being
rest in certainty
